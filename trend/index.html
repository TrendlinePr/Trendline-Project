The Problem

The session open is where challenges die.

Price sweeps a session high or low, takes out your stop, then reverses and runs without you. It happens at the same times every day — and it's where most prop challenges quietly fall apart.

01

The fakeout

The big players push price just past the level to trigger stops, then reverse. You're out at the exact wrong spot.

02

The chase

You jump back in late, widen your stop, and break your own rule — right when the real move starts.

03

The blown limit

A few of those and your drawdown limit is gone. The challenge fee with it. No plan, no edge, just reacting.

The Solution

One session map. Two edges.

Quantum STS marks each session's high and low, then gives you two mechanical ways to trade off them — and draws the full trade before price arrives.

Edge 1 · Fade the fakeout

Price sweeps past a session high or low to grab stops, then snaps back. Rest a limit at the level and catch the reversal.

Edge 2 · Ride the breakout

Price breaks the first range after the open and runs. Enter the break or retest, exit on a fixed target or when the trend flips.

1

Levels drawn from the last session

When a session closes, its high and low set the entry levels for the next one — automatically, across all five sessions.

2

R:R box painted before price arrives

Each level gets a take-profit zone and a stop zone, sized your way. The whole trade is laid out in advance — nothing to calculate in the moment.

3

Rest your order, let alerts fire

Drop a limit at the level and walk away, or read the chart and time it yourself. Alerts tell you the moment price hits.
