FAQ

Before you buy

Can I cancel anytime?+

Yes. Cancel whenever you like and you keep access until the end of your billing period. Annual subscribers can cancel to stop auto-renewal at the end of their year.

Do you offer refunds?+

No. You get instant access to proprietary software, so all purchases are non-refundable. Try the free Quantum Ribbon Lite on TradingView and review the live examples and demo videos before you buy.

How do I get access after paying?+

You'll get an email confirmation. Reply with your TradingView username and access is granted within 24 hours — the tools appear in your "Invite-Only Scripts" folder on TradingView.

Do I need a paid TradingView plan?+

No. All Pro Suite tools run on the free TradingView plan.

Can I buy just one side — Session or Trend?+

Yes. Quantum STS (Session) and Quantum Ribbon Pro (Trend) are sold separately. The Pro Suite simply bundles both for less than buying them individually.

Can I upgrade or switch plans later?+

Yes. You can move up to the Pro Suite or change your billing at any time.

What's the difference between monthly, annual, and lifetime?+

Monthly bills each month and cancels anytime. Annual is paid once a year at a lower effective rate. Lifetime is a single payment for permanent access — no recurring billing.
