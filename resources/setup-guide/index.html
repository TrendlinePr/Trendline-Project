Choose your plan
Starter for beginners. Pro for the complete toolkit.
→
Go to the pricing section and choose between QR Starter or QR Pro.
→
Click the plan button — you'll be taken to a secure checkout page powered by LemonSqueezy.
→
Complete your payment. You'll receive a receipt by email immediately after.

  • QR Starter indicator
  • QR Starter Backtester
QR Pro — 4 tools

  • TP RSI Pro

2
Submit your TradingView username
This is how we grant you access to your tools.
1
After purchase, a confirmation will appear with a "Get Access Here" button — click it.
2
You'll land on our setup page. Enter your TradingView username and the email you used at checkout.
3
Hit Submit — that's it. We'll activate your access shortly. You'll also receive a confirmation email where you can access your order.
3
Access your tools on TradingView
Once we've activated your access, here's where to find your tools.
1
Open TradingView and go to any chart.
2
Click the Indicators button at the top of the chart.
3
Select the Invite-Only Scripts tab on the left side of the panel.
4
Your tools will appear here. Click any of them to add to your chart.
5
If you don't see them yet — refresh TradingView or wait a moment and check again. Access is usually activated within a few hours of submitting your username.
