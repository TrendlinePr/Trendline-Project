Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Do you offer a free trial?+

Quantum Ribbon Lite (free version) is available on TradingView for testing the core ribbon system. If you're ready to go further, QR Starter is our entry-level paid plan, while QR Pro includes the full suite of advanced features including multi-timeframe confirmation, the Multi-Instrument Scanner, Backtester, and custom alerts.

Does this work on the free TradingView plan?+

Yes! Quantum Ribbon Pro works perfectly on the FREE TradingView plan. You do not need to upgrade your TradingView account.

What's the difference between Starter and Pro?+

QR Starter is a streamlined version of the ribbon system — ideal if you're new to systematic trading. It includes the core indicator and its own backtester. QR Pro is the complete system: additional confirmation layers (TP RSI, multi-timeframe, quantum range filter), the multi-instrument scanner, session filter, and advanced bot alerts. If you outgrow Starter, you can upgrade to Pro at any time.

What's the difference between Monthly and Annual?+

Both Monthly and Annual plans include exactly the same Pro features. Annual billing offers a significant saving compared to paying month to month. QR Starter is available on a monthly basis only.

Can I cancel anytime?+

Yes. Monthly subscribers can cancel anytime and retain access until the end of the billing period. Annual subscribers can cancel to stop auto-renewal at the end of their year.

What's included in the subscription?+

QR Pro includes: Quantum Ribbon Pro indicator, TP RSI Pro indicator, QR Pro Multi-Instrument Scanner, Strategy Backtester, complete video tutorials and setup guides, priority Discord support, and all future updates. QR Starter includes the QR Starter indicator and its dedicated Backtester.

How do I get access after subscribing?+

After subscribing, you will receive an email confirmation. Email us your TradingView username and access will be granted within 24 hours. All tools will appear in your "Invite-Only Scripts" folder on TradingView. Check your spam folder if you don't see confirmation emails.

Do you offer refunds?+

No. Due to the digital nature of this product (instant access to proprietary software), we do not offer refunds. Please review the Lite version, live examples, and demo videos carefully before subscribing.

Is this guaranteed to make me profitable?+

No. This is a tool to assist your trading decisions, not a guarantee of profits. Past performance does not guarantee future results. Always use proper risk management and never trade with money you cannot afford to lose.

What platforms does this work on?+

TradingView only. These are Pine Script indicators and strategies designed exclusively for the TradingView platform.

Does this work on all timeframes and markets?+

Yes. Quantum Ribbon Pro works on any timeframe and any market (stocks, crypto, forex, futures, indices). The indicator generally performs better on higher timeframes (15m+) due to reduced noise and more reliable signals.

Can I use this with trading bots?+

Yes. The indicator includes direction-specific JSON alerts designed for webhook integration with popular bot platforms like 3Commas, Alertatron, and custom webhook services.

Do I need coding knowledge to use this?+

No. All tools are fully configured through TradingView's settings menu. No coding required.
